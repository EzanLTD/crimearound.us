Need Help?

Try here first.

Draw a shape on the map…

Using the tools on the left side of the map interface, you can draw a custom shape on the map to define the area that you are interested in.

…optionally limit your query…

When you’re done drawing, you can optionally limit the types of crimes that are returned by using the filters in the left hand panel. Currently, your query can be limited by date range, time of day, primary crime type, location type, and police beat.

…get a report

Once you’ve honed in on what it is that you want to look at, you can click on “Search” to see the results of your query on the map. Clicking on any of the dots on the map will reveal some details about that particular crime report. If you’re interested in getting the data you’re looking at in a tabular format, you can use the Download Report button Excel Spreadsheet of everything you’re looking at. Have a use for other formats? You can submit an issue over here and tell me what you’re after.
